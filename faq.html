Frequently Asked Questions



What am I agreeing to when I open an account?
  • We do not require much personal information from you, but we do require that any information you provide is correct and true.
  • The most important piece of information currently required is your age. We restrict explicit content to users above 18. See the answers to the questions below for more information about this policy
  • For more information, please visit our Privacy Policy

Why do I need to create an account to see the content?
We require everyone to create accounts for two reasons:
  1. We want to be able to ensure that those taking time to exhibit work will be seen by real people who belong a community of like-minded individuals. If all the content was public, anyone could see it, public search crawlers would find it, and we could not ensure that copyrights could be enforced.
  2. We do not want to restrict the type of content beyond a couple of caveats (below), which means that some adult content may be posted and we need to restrict this content to appropriate ages (above), but want all of our members to see any content that is posted to ensure a community atmosphere.

What types of content can I post?
With few exceptions, you can post whatever you want! This includes video, images, audio, and text. The only restrictions we enforce is that we do not allow any content that incites violence or hate against others and we don't allow any content to be reposted that someone else has copyrighted. Before posting, please ask yourself the following questions:
  • Will (or may) this content cause harm to others?
  • Does someone else own the rights to this content?

If the answer to any of the above is "yes," please refrain from posting the content so that we can ensure a community of productive and original content.

If I post content here, do you own the rights to said content?
No! We will never endorse any policy that takes ownership of the content users post. We strongly recommend you take measures to copyright any content before you post and your copyright will be retained after posting. Please immediately share with us any suggestions you may have about how we can best protect your copyrighted material.

Please send any other questions you may have to info@seekexhibit.com or post in the forum. Thank you for your interest and we look forward to your contributions.
